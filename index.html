About Me

Hey, I am Sharad and I am a data analyst based out of San Francisco. I am motivated by the belief that data can be used to make better decisions. I make it easier for people to understand the code and use data efficiently. I play exceptionally well with Data Scientists and Product managers and can easily learn new concepts on the fly. I use Python primarily and believe in open source software. I recently received an MSBA from UC Davis Graduate School of Management with a focus on Analytics and Strategy. By day I analyze large chunks of data as an Analyst and by night I am a self-taught chef in my kitchen. I pen my thoughts (usually in lowercase) in my blog that I painstakingly curate which you can read here. I also love hiking, clicking photographs, playing a bunch of musical instruments, and drinking coffee/tea.
